The Problem

"Only real acts of goodness for fellow human beings deserve to generate value."

The Humanitarian Value Paradox

A volunteer who risks their life distributing food after an earthquake is rewarded with nothing. Meanwhile, a speculative meme token creator can accumulate life-changing wealth in hours. The disconnect between value creation and value capture is not a market inefficiency it is a structural failure of how money itself is defined.

The Opacity of Traditional Charity

Administrative overhead consumes 30–40% of donations before any aid reaches beneficiaries. Donors have no cryptographically verifiable proof that their contribution produced a real outcome. Corruption and fund diversion are chronic, particularly in crisis zones with weak institutional controls.

The Missing Infrastructure Layer

No existing infrastructure can simultaneously verify that a real humanitarian action occurred, attribute it to a unique real human, and automatically compensate that human without any intermediary making a discretionary decision. HAVEN builds this layer from first principles.

The Protocol Response

HAVEN replaces the trust gap with mathematical proof. Every VELD token in existence represents a cryptographically signed, AI-verified, on-chain record of a genuine humanitarian action a currency backed not by gold or debt, but by the accumulation of verified human kindness.

Core Innovation Proof of Beneficial Action

PoBA Mechanism

A Third
Consensus
Paradigm

Traditional blockchains ask participants to prove work or prove stake. HAVEN introduces a third paradigm: Proof of Beneficial Action.

PoBA is a cryptographic consensus mechanism that mints new VELD tokens only when a verified, real-world humanitarian action has been performed by a real and unique human. It is not self-reported. It is an AI-mediated multi-layer verification process backed by computer vision, behavioral analysis, GPS geofencing, and zero-knowledge cryptography.

"Saving the world is the only mining that matters."

Every verified action produces an Impact Score the quantitative measure that determines the VELD reward computed across three independent dimensions: Physical, Context, and Network layers.

Layer PoB-01
Physical Layer

Measures direct, tangible effort. Base weight by action type. Scaled by verified beneficiary count from AI vision not self-report. Multiplied by effort hours. Crisis Zone Multiplier up to 2.0× for volunteers within 200km of an active GDACS event.

Layer PoB-02
Context Layer

Measures situational risk and urgency. Urgency multipliers: CRITICAL 2.0×, HIGH 1.6×, MEDIUM 1.3×, LOW 1.0×. Time of action and environmental conditions factor into contextual scoring. Geofenced against live UN crisis data.

Layer PoB-03
Network Layer

Measures reputation and commitment history. Volunteer reputation from ReputationLedger. Cross-Temporal Chain Bonus: +5% per consecutive linked event, capped at +30%. Long-term volunteers accumulate governance and scoring advantages.

From Action to Token

User Journey

The complete, transparent flow from a volunteer opening the HAVEN application to receiving VELD tokens in their wallet. Every step is auditable. No step involves a human administrator making a discretionary judgment.

01
Identity Registration

Connect a Web3 wallet and register with the SovereignID smart contract, which issues a non-transferable Soulbound Identity NFT storing a biometric hash, country ISO code, and W3C DID document on IPFS ensuring one wallet per real person. New volunteers require vouching from three existing verified humans.

02
Evidence Submission

Select an action category and urgency level. Record effort hours, beneficiary count, and GPS coordinates. Capture a live photo via the dApp's camera module cryptographically watermarked with a SHA-256 hash at capture time. EXIF metadata validated for freshness and GPS consistency.

03
SATIN Verification

Evidence enters the SATIN oracle pipeline no human administrator involved. YOLOv8 counts people and objects in the photo. LLaVA analyzes the visual scene. Claimed parameters are cross-referenced against AI analysis. Behavioral fingerprinting, geofencing, ELA, and deduplication applied simultaneously.

04
ZK Signing & Minting

SATIN generates a Zero-Knowledge commitment proof a SHA-3 hash of beneficiary identity with random salt. The oracle signs the impact score, reward amount, and ZK proof via ECDSA. The BenevolenceVault verifies on-chain and calls the Avalanche NativeMinter to send VELD directly to the volunteer's wallet. Seconds.

Deflationary Flywheel

HAVEN's token economy is designed as a self-reinforcing deflationary flywheel. Three compounding mechanisms ensure circulating supply contracts over time as network activity and humanitarian impact grow.

Mechanism I
Donation Burns

VELD donated to the CrisisFund is effectively removed from circulating supply. 95% is locked in the crisis treasury and distributed as direct aid never returned to the market. Only a 5% Reflex Bonus is minted back to the generous donor.

Mechanism II
Idle Token Decay

A 2% annual burn rate applies to tokens held dormant for more than one year. This continuously reduces supply from inactive holders and incentivises token circulation, governance participation, and reinvestment into the protocol's mission.

Mechanism III
Crisis-Responsive Supply

During peacetime, the suffering index falls, minting caps tighten, and circulating supply grows more slowly naturally increasing scarcity and value as demand from volunteers and donors continues to expand the network.

Community Governance & Quadratic Benevolence

DAO Architecture

HAVEN's governance is designed to be unseizable. No single actor including StoneBridge Intelligence can unilaterally control the protocol. Voting power is determined by demonstrated humanitarian commitment, not capital concentration.

I
Quadratic Benevolence Voting

A custom voting formula designed to prevent plutocratic takeover. Diminishing returns on both actions and tokens doubling either does not double your power. Long-term commitment earns a growing tenure bonus. Pure token wealth contributes only 30% of voting power, making it mathematically impossible for a token whale to overrule a community of experienced volunteers.

Power = sqrt(impact_events) × (1 + tenure_days / 365 × 0.1)
+ sqrt(tokens_held) × 0.3
II
Proposal Eligibility & Rules

Creating a proposal requires at least 10 verified impact events governance is accessible only to those who have demonstrated real commitment. Proposals expire after 14 days. Quorum requires 10% of eligible voters. Standard proposals pass at a dynamic threshold of 40–51%. Constitutional proposals changes to the anti-Sybil core, oracle wallet list, or minimum PoBA threshold require a 2/3 supermajority and can never lower the minimum score below defined floors.

III
Community Stream Review

When SATIN's confidence falls below the 0.30 threshold, submissions are not rejected they route to the Community Stream, a live feed of pending cases requiring human review. Only CHAMPION-tier volunteers (500+ cumulative impact score) may serve as reviewers. A minimum of 3 Champion votes resolve each ambiguous case. Reviewers who consistently vote in the minority see their review privileges gradually reduced.

IV
Achievement & Reputation System

The ReputationLedger maintains nine badge tiers: FIRST_STEP · HELPER · DEDICATED · CHAMPION · LEGEND · HIGH_IMPACT · PERFECT · CENTURY · TITAN. The leaderboard is maintained on-chain as a sorted array updated by a KEEPER_ROLE (DAO-controlled). Confirmed Sybil attackers are permanently banned from score updates.

Security & Anti-Fraud Systems

Adversarial Threat Model

HAVEN's adversarial threat model assumes rational actors will attempt to extract VELD without performing genuine humanitarian actions. Every layer is designed to make fraud economically irrational.

Image Integrity
Multi-Layer Image Verification

SHA-256 deduplication rejects identical images. Perceptual hashing (dHash) detects edited or compressed copies within a Hamming distance threshold. Error Level Analysis flags digitally composited regions. EXIF metadata validates timestamp freshness and GPS consistency. Live capture enforcement prevents pre-taken photos.

Smart Contract Security
Contract-Level Hardening

ABI encoding uses abi.encode not abi.encodePacked eliminating hash collision vulnerabilities. Anti-replay protection via unique event ID and nonce mappings. OpenZeppelin ReentrancyGuard on all value-transferring functions. Role-based access control with ORACLE_ROLE, DAO_ADMIN_ROLE, VAULT_ROLE, KEEPER_ROLE. Pausable circuit breaker enables DAO to halt minting without a contract upgrade.

Governance Security
Constitutional Immutables

The anti-Sybil verification core, oracle wallet addresses, and minimum PoBA threshold are Constitutional changeable only by 2/3 supermajority and never reducible below current values. Quadratic dampening prevents token concentration from translating to governance capture. Only proven humanitarian actors can create proposals.

Identity Security
Soulbound Identity SovereignID

Each wallet holds exactly one non-transferable SovereignID NFT storing a W3C DID, biometric hash, ISO country code, and verified human status. New identities require social vouching from three existing verified humans creating a trust graph computationally expensive for Sybil attackers to fake. Vouchers face slashing if proven to have endorsed a Sybil.

Zero-Knowledge Proofs
Privacy-Preserving Verification

When SATIN approves a submission, the oracle generates a ZK commitment proof a SHA-3 hash of beneficiary identity combined with a random salt and public parameters. The beneficiary's identity is mathematically committed to the chain without ever being revealed. Roadmap includes full Circom/Groth16 ZK circuit implementation.

AI Vision Pipeline
YOLOv8 + LLaVA Verification

YOLOv8 computer vision counts the number of people and objects visible in submitted photos. LLaVA multimodal AI produces a natural language description of the visual scene. The oracle cross-references claimed parameters against AI visual analysis claiming 500 beneficiaries when the image shows 5 results in automatic rejection or proportional score reduction.
